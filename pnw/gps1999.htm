1999 Oregon GPS Campaign

RPI participants: Rob McCaffrey, Peter Zwick, Hanya Zwick, Cheryl Johnson, Charles Williams, Colleen Stevens

OSU participants: Chris Goldfinger, Joel Johnson, Yongdong Zhou, John Nabelek

The map below shows our 1999 campaign network occupied from Aug. 6 - 20 along with USGS campaign sites and continuous sites of the PANGA array. We occupied 54 sites (green and blue in map) with an average occupation time of 19 hours. HANS, GRND, and YAMB were run as semi-continuous, unattended sites for several days each.
